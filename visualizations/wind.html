Wind

The visualization above reflects the a city's wind speed in relation to its latitude. The data reflects that, on average, most cities experienced wind speeds lower than 10 mph. Additionally, cities closer to the equator were less likely to have high wind speeds, while those at higher latitudes (60+) were more likely to experience higher windspeeds.
